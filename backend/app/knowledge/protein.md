# Protein

Protein supports muscle repair, recovery, and satiety. A practical target for active
people is often 1.6 to 2.2 grams per kilogram of body weight per day, adjusted for
training volume, calorie intake, and preference.

Good sources include eggs, chicken, fish, lean beef, Greek yogurt, milk, tofu,
lentils, beans, whey, casein, and plant protein powders. Spread protein across
three to five meals when possible.
